Mom's Hot Buns

Ingredients:

  • 2c. Warm Water
  • ¼ c. Sugar
  • ¼ c. Oil
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 2 Eggs (1 for mixture & 1 for egg wash)
  • 1 Tbsp Yeast
  • 5-7c. All Purpose Flour
  • Optional – Pink Himalayan Sea Salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine water and sugar together in a bowl and wisk
  3. Add Oil, Salt and Egg into bowl and wisk together.
  4. Sprinkle the Yeast over ingredients and lightly wisk in the yeast.
  5. Cover with a warm dishcloth and let mixture set for 5-10 minutes. (This will allow the Yeast to activate).
  6. Remove dishcloth and begin adding the Flour one cup at a time, and mix together.
  7. Once dough begins to form then place it on a clean surface and begin kneading it for up to 10 minutes, adding flour as needed.
  8. Form into a ball and place in a greased bowl
  9. Cover with plastic wrap and a warm wet towel over the top of the bowl.
  10. Let proof in a warm location for 50 minutes.
  11. Optional-Punch down and let rise a second proof.
  12. Once proofed, roll dough onto a sheet pan making individual buns (makes up to 24 rolls).
  13. Cover with greased plastic wrap and let rise 15-20 minutes.
  14. Make the egg wash, mix remaining egg with 1 Tbsp water.
  15. Using a basting brush, brush egg wash on each bun.
  16. Optional-Sprinkle pink Himalayan over the buns.
  17. Bake in pre-heated oven for 20-22 minutes.
