Case Study: Animal Crossing New Horizons Interactive Field Guide

Concept & Inspiration

The idea behind this Field Guide was to create a cozy, user-friendly resource inspired by Animal Crossing’s warmth and charm.

Visual Design Decisions

  • Soft greens and browns for natural harmony.
  • Rounded buttons and friendly typography.
  • Simple navigation and consistent margins.

Layout & Structure

The design emphasizes clear hierarchy and easy scanning with a sidebar for navigation and a scrollable main content column.

Reflection & Next Steps

Building this project improved my skills in responsive design and accessible color usage. Next, I’d explore adding interactivity through JavaScript.
